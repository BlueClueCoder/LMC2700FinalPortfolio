What I've Learned

Recently, I've really tried to push myself to paint with purpose, and while I still don't think my work is great, I've definitely seen an improvement. Here is what I've learned:

  • Prime every model with black or white primer before painting
  • Paint one color at a time, and don't go back for mistakes until you've cycled through each color once
  • Don't worry if your base coat looks flat. Washes and hilights will fix this.
  • Use hilights at the very end to pick out one or two important pieces of your miniature
  • Don't over apply your spray finish, or your model will have a "wet" look
